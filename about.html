Local • Trusted • Professional About Linda’s Cleaning Services

Linda’s Cleaning Services is a trusted, local cleaning business based in Trowbridge, Wiltshire, built on over 10 years of professional experience and strong word-of-mouth recommendations.

What started as helping local residents with regular household cleaning has naturally grown into a reliable service supporting a wide range of clients — including elderly and vulnerable individuals who value care, consistency, and trust in their home.

Linda currently runs the business personally, ensuring every client receives a high standard of cleaning with a friendly, dependable, and no-nonsense approach. Many clients are long-term and trust Linda with regular access to their homes, knowing their space will always be treated with care and respect.

Services include regular domestic cleaning, deep cleans, end of tenancy cleaning, one-off cleans, and light non-medical home support where required. Linda proudly serves Trowbridge and surrounding towns and villages across Wiltshire, with flexible scheduling and a genuine commitment to helping create happy, comfortable homes.
