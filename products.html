Regular or one-off Cleaning Services

Services include:

  • Regular domestic cleaning (weekly / fortnightly)
  • Deep cleans (kitchens, bathrooms, full refresh)
  • End of tenancy cleaning
  • One-off cleans
  • Light home support (non-medical), where required
  • Optional extras: ironing, organising, and more

Friendly, reliable & thorough What We’ll Ask

To give an accurate quote (or confirm a booking request), we’ll usually ask:

  • Your postcode (Trowbridge / Wiltshire area)
  • Type of clean (regular, deep, end of tenancy, one-off)
  • Approx. home size (beds/baths) and any priority areas
  • Preferred days/times and frequency
  • Anything helpful to know (pets, parking, key safe, etc.)

If you’re unsure what you need, just message — we’ll guide you.
