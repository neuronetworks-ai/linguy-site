Reliable • Friendly • No-nonsense How Linda Can Help

Linda’s Cleaning Services provides trusted domestic cleaning and light non-medical home support in Trowbridge, Wiltshire and surrounding areas. With over 10 years of professional experience, the service is built on long-term clients, word-of-mouth recommendations, and attention to detail.

Services include regular domestic cleaning (weekly/fortnightly), deep cleans, end of tenancy cleaning, and one-off cleans. Linda is also experienced supporting elderly and vulnerable clients, offering a caring, respectful approach and consistent standards.

To request a quote, please share your postcode, the type of clean you need, and any priority areas. Bookings are confirmed after review to ensure the right time and service for your home.
